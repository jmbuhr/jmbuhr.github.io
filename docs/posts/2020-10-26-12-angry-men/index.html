12 Angry Men

BY REGINALD ROSE

By Jannik Buhr in theater project

May 1, 2018

Teaser

A young man is accused of having killed his father, the evidence points in this direction, the witnesses corroborate the story against him. His fate rests in the hand of a jury - twelve men and women to decide if he should be executed. Eleven jurors are certain he is the murderer, but for one some questions remain open. This Anglistic Seminar performance approaches a modern classic from a fresh perspective, drawing the audience into the moral conundrum.
